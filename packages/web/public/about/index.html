About LAF

LAF is a live audio streaming platform. Broadcast your audio in real time or tune in to live stations from around the world.

Broadcast

Create an account, set up a channel, and go live. Stream audio from your microphone to anyone listening. You can manage your channels, set cover art, and chat with your audience.

Live Stations

Browse stations that are currently live. Click to listen, sign in to participate in live chat, and enjoy real-time audio streaming.

Technology

LAF uses low-latency WebSocket streaming for real-time audio delivery.
